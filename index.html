Introduction to λ-calculus

λ-calculus is a formal system in mathematical logic and computer science for expressing computation based on function abstraction and application using variable binding and substitution. First formulated by Alonzo Church to formalize the concept of effective computability, lambda calculus found early successes in the area of computability theory. Lambda calculus is a conceptually simple universal model of computation.
